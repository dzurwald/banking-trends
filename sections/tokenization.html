Tokenization of Assets

Why it is Important

Asset tokenization represents a paradigm shift in how ownership rights are recorded and transferred, fundamentally altering traditional financial markets [106][107]. This technology transforms real-world assets into digital tokens on blockchain platforms, enabling fractional ownership, enhanced liquidity, and streamlined transaction processes [108]. By converting rights to assets into digital tokens, banks can democratize access to investment opportunities previously reserved for high‑net‑worth individuals and institutional investors [109][110].

Market Size / Opportunity

The global asset tokenization market demonstrates explosive growth potential. The market was valued at USD 5.60 billion in 2024 and is projected to grow from USD 6.62 billion in 2025 to USD 30.21 billion by 2034, exhibiting a CAGR of 18.4% [107]. Tokenized real‑world assets are expected to achieve a CAGR of 53%, soaring from US$600 billion in 2025 to US$18.9 trillion by 2033 [111][112].

Important Insights

In the global bond market, tokenization could cut operational costs by 40–60% [111]. For a US$5 million real estate fund, tokenization could unlock up to US$500 million in new capital inflow by lowering access thresholds and reduce annual admin and compliance costs by US$100–150 million over five years [111]. The technology enables 24/7 transactions, faster settlement, and programmable smart contracts that automate conditional transactions [113].

Success Stories

  • BlackRock BUIDL: Tokenized fund providing daily dividends and enhanced liquidity [110].
  • JPMorgan Chase: Moved beyond pilots to deploy real on‑chain tokenization products [112].
  • Hamilton Lane: Successfully implemented tokenized asset products via blockchain networks [112].
  • Mercado Bitcoin × Polygon Labs: Issued 340+ tokenized products totaling ≈$180M across private credit, fixed income, and revenue‑sharing products [112].
  • Goldman Sachs Digital Asset Platform: Comprehensive tokenization infrastructure for institutional clients [110].
