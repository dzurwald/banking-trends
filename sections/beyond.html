Beyond Banking

Why It Is Important

Banks in Central and Eastern Europe face increasing pressure to remain relevant in a world where digital-native companies are winning the customer relationship. Moving "beyond banking" means offering services that address more than just financial needs—integrating into everyday life and solving broader problems. Nearly 60% of banking customers expect their bank to provide lifestyle-related services in the next five years [124].

Market Size / Opportunity

“Beyond banking” strategies enable financial institutions to tap into new verticals:
— Digital lifestyle services projected to grow to $800+ billion globally by 2030 [124]
— Fintech super apps see 3–5× engagement vs. single-purpose banking apps [125]
— Increased cross-sell conversion: +17% when lifestyle services are bundled with financial products [126]

Important Insights

Platform Thinking Wins: Banks need to think like platforms, enabling others to build on top of their services through APIs and partner ecosystems [127].

Customer Lifecycle Integration: Addressing everyday moments—mobility, housing, health, education—deepens retention and lifetime value [124].

Trust Still Matters: Banks have higher consumer trust than Big Tech when it comes to managing data and identity, making them ideal stewards of integrated digital life services [128].

Success Stories

  • BBVA Valora View — Combines real estate search, price insights, and mortgage financing in one app [125].
  • YONO by SBI (India) — Lifestyle + banking app offering shopping, travel, education services, and loans in one ecosystem [129].
  • mBank’s mAccelerator — Incubates startups building beyond-banking services on top of the bank’s core APIs [130].
  • Intesa Sanpaolo’s XME Salute — Digital healthcare offering bundled with bank accounts and financing [131].

Ideas for CEE Banks

  • Banking + Mobility: Offer public transport passes, EV subscriptions, and car maintenance bundled with bank accounts.
  • Digital Wellness Stack: Provide insurance, fitness memberships, and mental health tools within mobile banking apps.
  • Home Ownership Journey: Create a digital journey combining real estate listings, price comparisons, legal help, and mortgage pre-approval.
  • Green Living Platform: Offer subsidies, grants, and loans for solar panels and energy retrofits—bundled with CO₂ tracking dashboards.
  • Youth Education Stack: Build embedded education micro-loans, scholarship finders, and job prep tools for students.
